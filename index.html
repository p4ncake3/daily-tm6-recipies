Breakfast Recipes

Banana Pancakes

Quick and healthy banana pancakes made in your TM6.

Ingredients:

  • 2 ripe bananas
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup oats
  • 1 tsp baking powder

Instructions:

  1. Blend all ingredients in TM6 until smooth.
  2. Pour batter onto a heated non-stick pan.
  3. Cook each side for 2-3 minutes until golden.
  4. Serve with fruits or syrup.
